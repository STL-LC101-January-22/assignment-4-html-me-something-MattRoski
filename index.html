Grand Teton National Park

We began our vacation at Grand Teton Nation Park.

The mountains themselves

Living in the midwest my whole life, this was the first time I saw a proper mountain range.

The view from the mountain

I could not believe how far I could see...

Boat ride

We took a boat over to a secluded and hardly touched area in the park.

Waterfalls!

We followed the path and it led us to a waterfall!

This waterfall was found off a small path on the side of the road.

Buffalo

We met and befriended a Buffalo. He followed us for a few minutes. Kind of made me nervous to be honest.

BEARS!

That chunk of fur is a real grizzly bear. Everyone got out of their cars to admire this beast. You might think that's not very smart, and admittedly, it really isn't... But atleast I was smart enough to stand behind the person in the red jacket.

To make matters more exciting, my uncle who was driving our van, lost the keys while we were outside! Top five scares for me easy.

Canyon

The final day was to see the Grand Canyon of Yellow Stone. This is probably the best picture I've taken in my life.
